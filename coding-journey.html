Early Life and Introduction to Coding

I was born on May 13, 1998, in Islampur, West Bengal, India. From a young age, I was captivated by technology, spending hours exploring computers. This early exposure ignited my curiosity about how software worked, leading me to teach myself programming languages.

During my childhood, I discovered the basics of coding through online tutorials and coding platforms. I began with HTML and CSS, quickly grasping the concepts of web design. As I experimented with creating simple web pages, my interest grew, and I soon delved into JavaScript to add interactivity to my projects.

Professional Experience

After gaining confidence in my coding skills, I sought professional opportunities. I secured a role at Hiveloop Technology Pvt Ltd (Udaan.com) as an Executive, where I applied my coding knowledge to streamline hub operations and logistics processes. Utilizing Microsoft Office Suite and Google Workspace, I developed custom formula functions in Excel and Google Sheets using JavaScript. This allowed me to automate data calculations, improve efficiency in data management, and enhance reporting accuracy, making it easier for the team to analyze performance metrics and make informed decisions.

Later, I joined Instakart Services Pvt Ltd (Flipkart) as a Team Leader (Off Role). In this position, I led a team and utilized my analytical skills to identify areas for improvement in logistics. I continued to harness my coding expertise to create advanced custom formulas and scripts that optimized workflows in Excel and Google Sheets. My efforts not only improved the speed and accuracy of data analysis but also contributed to effective resource allocation and performance tracking, further solidifying my passion for technology and problem-solving.
